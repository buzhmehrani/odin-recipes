Classic Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe features layers of tender pasta, rich Bolognese meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and plenty of melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 28 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tsp dried basil
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook beef with onion and garlic until browned.
  3. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, and herbs. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Cook noodles according to package directions.
  5. Mix ricotta with mozzarella, Parmesan, and egg.
  6. Spread sauce in a baking dish, layer noodles and cheese mixture.
  7. Repeat layers and top with cheese.
  8. Bake for 25–30 minutes. Let rest before serving.
