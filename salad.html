Garden Fresh Salad

Description

A refreshing salad featuring crisp lettuce, juicy cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and a tangy vinaigrette. Perfect as a light meal or a healthy side dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups mixed greens or romaine lettuce
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup black olives, sliced
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and dry all vegetables thoroughly.
  2. Chop or slice ingredients as desired.
  3. In a large bowl, combine lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, and onions.
  4. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  5. Drizzle dressing over the salad and toss gently.
  6. Top with olives and feta cheese if desired.
  7. Serve immediately for maximum freshness.
